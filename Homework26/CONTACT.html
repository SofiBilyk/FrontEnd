CONTACT The normal temperature for a brain is 36,9-37,8 degrees. If we overheat,firstly excitation processes will be activated in our central nervous system. Moreover, if there isn't temperature reduction, the central nervous system will wear out and lose its energy. As a result, it's hard to think and to make decisions, we can't concentrate. So, What can we do to keep productivity in the summer heat? Make yourself cooled After riding in suffocating public transport or being on a street for a long time, to have a breezy shower is the best decision. If you don't have any opportunity like this, you need to wet your hands to elbows, but to the shoulders is better. Also you should wash your face with cold water and put a wet handkerchief on your neck. These actions can reduce the temperature a little. Wear the natural light clothes In the heat our clothes must be done with natural fabrics and be free. If you know that you are going to be under the sun for a long time you should wear things, which cover as many skin parts as you can. Otherwise it's a high possibility to get sun burns. Take care about your water balance The World Health Organization recommended drinking 30 milliliters of water per 1 kilogram of body weight. So, in the hot summer we need more, how much more depends on your organism. Forget about tea or coffee These drinks cause water loss, that's why you should choose just usual fresh water.
